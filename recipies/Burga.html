Burga

This is a famous delicious Food

    Ingredients:
  • Ground beef (preferably around 80% lean)
  • Hamburger buns
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Optional toppings (lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, pickles, etc.)
  • Optional condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, BBQ sauce, etc.)
    Steps
  1. Season ground beef with salt and pepper, shape into patties.
  2. Grill or pan-fry patties until desired doneness (3-4 min each side for medium).
  3. Toast hamburger buns if desired.
  4. Place patties on buns, add toppings and condiments.
