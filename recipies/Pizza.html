Pizza

This is a famous Italian Food

    Ingredients:
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Green Peppers
  • Olives
  • Basil
    Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Prepare the dough.
  3. Roll out the dough into a round shape.
  4. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  6. Add your favorite toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, green peppers, olives).
  7. Sprinkle some basil over the toppings.
  8. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool slightly.
  10. Slice and serve the pizza hot.
