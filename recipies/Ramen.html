Burga

This is a famous delicious Food

    Ingredients:
  • Ramen noodles
  • Chicken or vegetable broth
  • Flavor packet or seasoning (soy sauce, miso paste, etc.)
  • Sliced pork, chicken, or tofu
  • Green onions
  • Spinach
  • Mushrooms
  • Seaweed
  • Soft-boiled egg
  • Sesame seeds
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
    Steps
  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add the ramen noodles to the boiling water.
  3. Cook the noodles for 3-4 minutes or until tender.
  4. While the noodles are cooking, prepare the broth.
  5. Heat the broth in a separate pot and bring it to a simmer.
  6. Add the flavor packet or your own seasonings to the broth.
  7. Once the noodles are cooked, drain them and add them to the broth.
  8. Top with your choice of protein (e.g., sliced pork, chicken, tofu).
  9. Add vegetables and garnishes (e.g., green onions, spinach, mushrooms, seaweed).
  10. Let the ramen sit for a minute to absorb the flavors.
  11. Serve the ramen hot and enjoy!
